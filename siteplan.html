Cybex Gaming Store - Site Plan

A blueprint for the ultimate gaming experience website.

1. Site Name

Cybex Gaming Store

The name of the site is Cybex Gaming Store. It is a name that suggests a high quality, futuristic gaming experience, appealing to both casual and hardcore gamers.

Optional domain availability: cybexgaming.com

2. Site Purpose

The Cybex Gaming Store website purpose is to be the premier online destination for gamers that seek latest games, high-performance gaming gears and setup, and a vibrant community where they get to know each other and also host tournament. It provides comprehensive product listings, membership benefits, community event information, and useful articles to enhance the gaming lifestyle.

3. Scenarios

  • "I'm new to PC gaming. Where can I find recommendations for a good beginner gaming headset and controller?"
  • "I heard about a local Valorant tournament. How can I sign up or find out more details about upcoming events?"
  • "What are the benefits of becoming a Cybex member, and how do I apply?"
  • "I'm looking for a new gaming lounge or VR center in Lagos. Does Cybex have a list or recommendations?"

4. Color Schema

Our color schema is chosen to reflect a modern, energetic, and immersive gaming experience, with high contrast for readability.

  • Primary Color (#1f1f2e): Used for backgrounds, header, footer, and main content sections. Provides a dark, futuristic base.
  • Accent Color (#ff0055): Used for call-to-action buttons, active navigation links, headings, and key highlights. Provides a vibrant, energetic contrast.
  • Text Color (#ffffff): Used for primary text content to ensure high readability against dark backgrounds.
  • Background Color (#121212): The deepest dark for the overall page background, providing depth.

(These colors are applied within this site plan document for demonstration.)

5. Typography

We will use one clean, modern sans-serif font for optimal readability and a sleek aesthetic across the site.

  • Font Selection: 'Segoe UI', Arial, sans-serif
  • Application::
    • Headings (H1, H2, H3): 'Segoe UI' (or Arial/sans-serif fallback) for a modern, bold look.
    • Body Text & Paragraphs: 'Segoe UI' (or Arial/sans-serif fallback) for clear and legible content.
    • Navigation & Buttons: 'Segoe UI' (or Arial/sans-serif fallback) for a consistent and crisp interface.

(This font is applied to the entire body of this site plan document for demonstration.)
